VEME 2024 NGS Variant Calling

About the Project

VEME 2024 NGS Variant Calling is an educational project aimed at researchers, students, or professionals in genomics or bioinformatics. It focuses on providing comprehensive resources and training on Next-Generation Sequencing (NGS) and variant calling techniques. The project is designed to enhance understanding and skills in genomic data analysis.
